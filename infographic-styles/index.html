What is a NotebookLM style?

A NotebookLM style is a visual prompt that controls how NotebookLM (and other AI image generators) renders an infographic, slide background, or video-overview frame. This catalogue collects 140 reusable styles — from editorial print to dashboard, retro to neon — each with a ready-to-copy prompt. They work in NotebookLM video overviews and slide decks, and also in newer text-aware image models like ChatGPT Images 2 (April 2026) and similar tools.
