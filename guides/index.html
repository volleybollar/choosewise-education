Which AI guide should I read?

Five free guides for teachers and school leaders, each focused on a major AI surface that schools are already using: Microsoft Copilot, Google Gemini & NotebookLM, Apple Intelligence, Claude, and a pedagogical decision guide on whether students should use AI in class.
