The WISE Framework

The right tool at the right time.

Four questions that turn any "should we use this AI tool?" conversation into a structured decision — grounded in professional judgment, not vendor hype.

W — Weigh the learning goal

What are the students actually supposed to learn? Before any tool talk, name the goal in one sentence.

I — Inspect what the subject requires

Math, literature, history, crafts — each subject has its own epistemic demands. What does this subject uniquely require?

S — Select the right tool

Paper, book, digital, AI, or nothing at all. The right tool isn't the newest — it's the one that serves the goal and subject.

E — Evaluate the outcome

Did students learn what we said they'd learn? If yes, repeat. If no, don't default to "a better tool" — go back to W.
