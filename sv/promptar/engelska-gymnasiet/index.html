  1. Write a 200-word article in English about the impact of social media on teenagers for English 5 and create 5 comprehension questions focusing on main ideas and details.   2. Write 5 discussion questions for English 5 based on a 200-word article about the effects of climate change in English-speaking countries and focus on solutions and individual responsibility.   3. Write a 200-word excerpt from a dystopian novel set in a future English-speaking society for English 7 and create 5 questions that encourage students to analyze themes and symbolism. Generate a 150-word summary of Shakespeare’s Romeo and Juliet for English 6 and write 4 questions that focus on the characters’ motivations and the play’s cultural impact.
  4. Write a task for English 6 where students improve a 150-word text by adding descriptive adjectives and adverbs and provide 10 examples of descriptive words they can use. Samhällsfrågor
  5. Create a task for English 5 where students write a 200-word story about a magical object found in an English-speaking country and provide 5 descriptive words to inspire their writing.

Så här använder du dem

Klistra in prompten i en chattbot. Där du ser [hakparenteser], byt ut texten mot det som passar din situation. Dubbelkolla alltid svaren — och ladda aldrig upp personuppgifter eller känslig information.

Promptarna fungerar naturligt tillsammans med RÄTT-modellen: modellen hjälper dig avgöra om ett AI-verktyg hör hemma i klassrummet; promptarna hjälper dig använda det väl när svaret är ja.
