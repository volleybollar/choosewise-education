Who is Johan Lindström?

Johan Lindström is a Swedish education consultant with 28 years inside the school system — 10 as a teacher, 7 as a school leader, and 11 in EdTech. He runs JL Skolutveckling AB and choosewise.education, where he publishes the WISE Framework for Education and free practical material for teachers and school leaders. He's a recognized voice on AI and digitalization in Swedish education, focused on classroom reality rather than vendor hype.

About

Johan Lindström

I've spent 28 years inside Swedish schools. Ten as a teacher. Seven as a school leader. Eleven as head of education. Alongside my main job, I run JL Skolutveckling AB and the site choosewise.education.

The AI conversation in schools is dominated by a debate where the loudest voices are those most for or most against new technology. I built this site to add a balanced voice — grounded in classroom reality.

I believe the best tool isn't always the newest. Sometimes it's paper. Sometimes a book. Sometimes a digital tool. Sometimes an AI tool. The WISE Framework for Education is how I help teachers and school leaders decide which tool is right here and now — keeping professional judgment in focus.
