Johan Lindström

Johan Lindström is a former teacher and school leader in Sweden that currently works with schools all over Sweden. With 28 years of experience I've learnt a lot about what works and what doesn't work and what teachers and school leaders struggle with.

The AI conversation in schools is dominated by a debate where the loudest voices are those most for or most against new technology. I built this site to add a balanced voice — grounded in classroom reality.

I believe the best tool isn't always the newest. Sometimes it's paper. Sometimes a book. Sometimes a digital tool. Sometimes an AI tool. The WISE Framework for Education is how I help teachers and school leaders decide which tool is right here and now — keeping professional judgment in focus.
