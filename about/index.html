Johan Lindström

I've spent 27 years inside Swedish schools. Ten as a teacher. Six as a school leader. Eleven in EdTech at Advania Skolpartner. I run JL Skolutveckling AB and write at choosewise.education.

The AI conversation in schools is being dominated by people who have never taught a Tuesday. I built this site to add a balanced voice — grounded in classroom reality, not vendor slide decks.

I believe the best tool is often not the newest one. Sometimes it's paper. Sometimes it's a book. Sometimes it's silence. The WISE framework is how I help teachers and school leaders make that call — with professional judgment intact.
