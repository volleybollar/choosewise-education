Privacy

Privacy policy

Short version: this site collects your email only if you choose to download a gated guide. No tracking. No cookies for analytics. No third-party ads.

What we collect

Email addresses — only when you actively submit the form to download one of the AI-tool guides (Claude, Gemini/NotebookLM, Apple Intelligence, Copilot). We use MailerLite to store this list and send you the guide plus occasional updates about new guides.

Nothing else. No analytics, no cookies, no tracking pixels. Downloading the WISE handout, prompt packs, and NotebookLM styles does not require an email.

Your rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold by emailing hello@choosewise.education
  • Deletion: Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. Clicking it removes your address.
  • Correction: Contact us and we'll update any incorrect information.

Data location

MailerLite stores subscriber data on EU servers. They are GDPR compliant. See MailerLite's privacy policy for their practices.

Contact

Questions? hello@choosewise.education

Difficulty ratings on /ai-or-human/

If you choose to rate the difficulty of any test on the AI or human? page (1–6 scale, fully optional), your rating, the language version of the page, the test you rated, and your score on that test are sent anonymously to a Google Sheet owned by Johan.
