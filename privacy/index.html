Privacy

Privacy policy

Short version: this site collects your email only if you choose to submit it via a gated download form. No tracking. No cookies for analytics. No third-party ads.

What we collect

Email addresses — only when you actively submit a download form on this site. We use MailerLite to store the list and send you the requested material plus occasional updates.

Nothing else. No analytics, no cookies, no tracking pixels. The WISE handout, prompt packs, and NotebookLM styles do not require an email.

Your rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold by emailing hello@choosewise.education.
  • Deletion: Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. Clicking it removes your address.
  • Correction: Contact us and we'll update any incorrect information.

Data location

MailerLite stores subscriber data on EU servers. They are GDPR compliant. See MailerLite's privacy policy for their practices.

Difficulty ratings on /ai-or-human/

If you choose to rate the difficulty of any test on the AI or human? page (1–6 scale, fully optional), your rating, the language version of the page, the test you rated, and your score on that test are sent anonymously to a Google Sheet owned by Johan. No cookies, no IP addresses, no user agent, and no session ID are stored. The rating is used only to understand how difficult visitors find the exercises in aggregate.
