Superintendents

Five sample prompts from this prompt pack

Every prompt is a template with bracketed placeholders [replace this text] that you swap for your own context.

  1. Create a strategic plan to improve equity in teaching across all schools in the municipality, including three concrete measures and a timeline for follow-up.
  2. Give me five ideas for reducing the administrative burden on principals and teachers in our schools, with a focus on [specific process, e.g. documentation].
  3. Suggest three ways to support schools in meeting the requirements of the national curriculum (Lgr22) for [specific subject or goal] across our schools.
  4. Write a brief statement for local media about a success in our schools (e.g. [specific success]), with a call for collaboration.
  5. Type in the full prompt and answer the follow-up questions you receive from the chatbot. Remember not to include sensitive information!

How to use them

Paste the prompt into a chatbot. Where you see [bracketed text], replace it with what fits your situation. Always double-check output for accuracy — and never upload personal or sensitive data.

All prompts were originally developed in a Swedish school context. This means that some prompts may need to be adapted to the curriculum and context in your country.

These prompts pair with the WISE Framework for Education: the framework helps you decide whether to use an AI tool; the prompts help you use it well once you do.
