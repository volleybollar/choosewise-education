School Catering Staff

Five sample prompts from this prompt pack

Every prompt is a template with bracketed placeholders [replace this text] that you swap for your own context.

  1. Create a weekly menu for a [primary school] that follows the Swedish Food Agency's recommendations for school meals, including three vegetarian options and a nutritional breakdown.
  2. Give me five ideas for celebrating School Meals Day or a similar event at a [primary school], with one activity for each.
  3. Suggest three ways to collaborate with students at a [primary school] to plan a themed day for school meals (e.g. [specific theme]), with one idea for each.
  4. Create a list of five quick hygiene checks to carry out in the school kitchen at a [primary school] before service, with a focus on [specific area, e.g. work surfaces].
  5. Create a list of three short ways to show appreciation to students who help out in the dining hall at a [primary school], with an example for each.

How to use them

Paste the prompt into a chatbot. Where you see [bracketed text], replace it with what fits your situation. Always double-check output for accuracy — and never upload personal or sensitive data.

All prompts were originally developed in a Swedish school context. This means that some prompts may need to be adapted to the curriculum and context in your country.

These prompts pair with the WISE Framework for Education: the framework helps you decide whether to use an AI tool; the prompts help you use it well once you do.
